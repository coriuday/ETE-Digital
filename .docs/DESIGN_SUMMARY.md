## 🎨 Design System

### Color Palette

- **Primary**: Custom Blue (#1132d4 - #3211d4 range)
- **Gradients**: Purple to Blue (professional corporate aesthetic)
- **Accents**: Secondary colors for badges and highlights
- **UI Elements**: Clean whites, grays for contrast

### Typography

- **Font Family**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Hierarchy**: Bold headings, medium weights for body text
- **Readability**: Excellent line-height and letter-spacing

### Components

- **Cards**: Rounded corners (8px - 12px), smooth shadows
- **Buttons**: Primary (solid), Secondary (outline), with hover states
- **Forms**: Clean inputs with validation states
- **Badges**: Pill-shaped status indicators
- **Progress**: Circular gauges, linear bars, sparklines
- **Charts**: Line, bar, donut with purple-blue gradients

### Effects

- **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass effects on cards
- **Shadows**: Multi-layered, soft shadows
- **Animations**: Smooth transitions, hover effects, micro-interactions
- **Gradients**: Purple-to-blue brand gradients throughout
